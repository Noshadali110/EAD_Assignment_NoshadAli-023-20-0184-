Noshad Ali Kalhoro

The Hult Prize challenges young people around the world to solve the planet's most pressing issues through social entrepreneurship. The winning team will get a one-million-dollar seed investment, as well as mentorship and guidance from the worldwide business community. The Hult Prize seeks to nurture the next generation of social entrepreneurs and, eventually, to build a start-up social company capable of fundamentally changing the world.
